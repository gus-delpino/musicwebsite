BIOGRAPHY

Who is Gustavo?

Having performed with world renowned artists such as Alejandro Sanz, Susana Baca, Joyce Moreno, and Rosa Pasos, Gustavo began his journey as a talented pianist at the age of 6 in Lima, Peru. At 11, he secured solo classical recitals around the town, beginning to make a name for himself among the urbanites. His talent got him noticed by the admissions committee of the National Conservatory of Music in Lima, where he auditioned and secured himself the only spot available for a pianist in the conservatory at the age of 17. There, he continued his classical training.

At 19, he moved to the United States and had the fortune of finding a group of musicians who piqued his interest in jazz, R&B, and Brazilian music, and deepened his appreciation and knowledge of Latin music. It was with these musicians that he began training and performing in styles other than classical.

After years of training and performing in a small town in middle America, he received a scholarship to attend the prestigious Berklee College of Music in Boston, MA. Here, he honed his jazz and Latin skills, and flexed his creative muscles in other styles. He has studied under Oscar Stagnaro, Alain Mallet, and Ray Santiago, among others. This led him to amazing opportunities to perform and record with many talented rising stars, as well as current household favorites.

In the past 4 years, he has recorded 5 full albums and participated in numerous sessions, performing in a variety of styles. Check out some samples in his portfolio
